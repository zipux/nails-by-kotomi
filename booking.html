Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a gel manicure take? +

A standard gel manicure takes approximately 60–75 minutes, including nail prep, colour application and LED curing. Adding nail art may extend the appointment.

Do I need to do anything before my appointment? +

Please arrive with clean nails — remove any existing polish or gel at home if possible. Avoid applying hand cream on the day, as it can affect adhesion. That's all!

Can I bring my own nail colour? +

Yes! You're welcome to bring your own gel or regular polish. Please let me know in advance so I can check compatibility with the products I use.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule? +

No problem — life happens! Please give at least 24 hours' notice via the booking system or by email. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a 50% charge.
